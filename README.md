# Hirafi
Description of Hirafi:

allow clients to seamlessly find and locate craftsmen near them to acquire services from them , and allow craftsmen to offer their services to the clients and easen transactions.

Detailed Description:
Functional/Non-Functional Requirements:

1.user authentication: allow clients and craftsmen to login using their email or phone number

2.personal archive for craftsmen: includes speciality , experience , ratings and personal exhibition.(العرض).

3.search and filtering system: allow clients to find craftsmen based on location/speciality.

4.ordering system: allow clients to offer jobs/Hiring statuses for craftsmen to apply for.

5.closed chat between clients and craftsmen.

6.online payment system: allow clients to pay craftsmen for their services online.

7.buying and selling marketplace of tools: allow craftsmen to browse and buy used/new tools / kits.

8.rating system: allow clients to rate craftsmen to motivate/give an overview of craftsmen.

9.real-time notifications: to notify users about new open jobs/messages or updates of the app.

more details on these are on the specification requirements document.

Tech Used:
A. Frontend: Flutter
B. Backend: firebase (Baas)
